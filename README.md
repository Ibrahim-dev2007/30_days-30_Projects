# 🚀 30 Days • 30 Frontend Projects

> Restarting my coding journey — one project at a time.

---

## 💭 Why This Challenge?

At some point, I felt like I had **forgotten coding**.  
Not completely… but enough to lose confidence.

Instead of waiting for motivation, I decided to **start again**.

This challenge is my way of:

- Revising fundamentals
- Rebuilding consistency
- Proving to myself that I still got it

---

## 🎯 My Commitment

👉 I will build **1 frontend project every day for 30 days**  
👉 I will share my progress publicly  
👉 No excuses. Just consistency.
